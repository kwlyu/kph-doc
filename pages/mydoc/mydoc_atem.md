## Hardware

We use an [ATEM 1 M/E Constellation HD](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/atemconstellation/techspecs/W-APS-25) as our main video switcher in Kracum. Applebaum has an [ATEM Mini Pro ISO](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/atemmini/techspecs/W-APS-15) as a secondary video switcher. Both switchers (and other Blackmagic products) can be controlled with [ATEM Software Control](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/atemmini/software). The only difference is that the ATEM Mini allows for ISO recording of all video/audio sources and streaming, whereas the Constellation is just a (very powerful) switcher. We will touch on both as needed.
